## Available Materials

### Introduction to AI
- Course overview and lecture slides
- Multiple summaries (English & Dutch)
- History of AI book summary
- Machine Learning intro lecture

### Introduction to Psychology
- Full lecture summaries and chapter summaries
- Individual lecture notes (consciousness, perception, learning)
- Mock exams with answer keys
- Weekly quizzes and textbook practice questions

### Intelligent Systems
- Formula sheets (1st and 2nd partial)
- Crash course slides with notes (2023)
- Fuzzy logic explanation slides
- Final exam 2023 Q1 solution with grading scheme

### Logic & Sets for AI
- Midterm and endterm crash course notes
- Logic and sets lecture notes
- Exercise sets and TA materials
- Summary of sets theory lecture slides

### Introduction to Python for AI
- Python basics cheat sheet and coding style guide
- Midterm practice notebook
- Workbook solutions (functions, conditionals, loops, exceptions)
- Lecture notebooks and recap slides

### History of AI
- Chapter summaries (chapters 1-12)
- Campbell-Kelly "Computer" book summaries
- Lecture notes (English & Dutch)

### Human-Computer Interaction
- Book summary and final summary
- Exam preparation materials

### Information Management
- Study material summary
- Exam questions and revision guide
- Business Information Management textbook (Chaffey & Wood)

### Modelling Human Behaviour (Dynamic Modelling)
- Course summaries and full exam prep summary
- Resit exams 2022 and 2023

### Computational Thinking
- Syllabus chapters 1-6 summary

### Project Intelligent Systems
- Schnapsen project files
